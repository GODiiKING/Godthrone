Attributes of Godthrone’s Cosmos

Godthrone Blessings
In the vast universe shaped by Godthrone, every being—mortal or divine—is defined by their Attributes. These characteristics govern their capabilities, growth, and influence within the many planes of existence. From the mortal Mere to the celestial Space Gods, these attributes reflect the core alignments of physical, mental, and metaphysical power.


Lore of Attributes

Attributes

Understanding the different alignments and their influence on characters:

Strength

Measuring physical power, will, tolerance.

"Through the breaking of bones and the shattering of wills, I rise, unyielding in my thirst for power."

Speed

Measuring agility, reflexes, balances.

"I move faster than the reaper’s blade, striking before my enemy even knows they’re already dead."

Stamina

Measuring endurance.

"I endure through the ages, my body a vessel of relentless pain, yet my will remains unbroken."

Magic

Measuring non-material elements (Fire – Air – Earth – Water).

"I bend the elements to my will, shaping the very forces of nature into instruments of destruction."

Wisdom

measuring Perception and Insight

"I see the world through the eyes of a predator, knowing the hidden weaknesses in every soul I encounter."

Creation

measuring (Debuff) 5th elements (Life / Death)

"From death, I craft anew—life and decay woven together in a cycle of eternal suffering."

Anti-Magic

measuring (Counter for Magic / Buff)

"I am the void that consumes magic itself, the silence that snuffs out all power and hope."

Arcane Magic

measuring (Stacks / Buff)

"I weave the threads of the arcane, stacking power upon power until the very fabric of reality tears beneath my hand."

Interplay Between Attributes

influence on characters
The Attributes do not exist in isolation. Each being's unique combination of strengths defines their role in the cosmos:


  • Strength and Stamina create formidable warriors.

  • Speed and Wisdom forge agile strategists and elusive rogues.

  • Magic and Anti-Magic pit casters against counters, balancing creation and suppression.

  • Creation and Arcane Magic lead to godlike mastery over life, death, and reality itself.

The universe of Godthrone thrives on the balance and tension between these forces, with every being—from the lowliest Mere to the mightiest Space God—striving to perfect their alignment and ascend toward ultimate power.
