Forbidden Sword

Dimensional Omnicide

++

(Ethereal Sword, Ice Sword, Toxic Sword)

Beyond the veil of mortal comprehension, when Ven first gazed upon reality and found it desperately inadequate, this weapon manifested as the physical embodiment of his omniversal oversight. Born from the fusion of three instruments of cosmic tyranny - the Ethereal Sword's dimensional transcendence, the Ice Sword's absolute stasis, and the Toxic Sword's corrupting dominion - it serves as reality's death sentence made manifest.

The Ethereal Sword grants the weapon existence across all planes simultaneously, allowing Ven's authority to phase through every barrier reality attempts to erect. It doesn't bypass dimensional walls - it simply refuses to acknowledge their right to exist, cutting through space-time itself with the casual contempt of absolute power.

The Ice Sword contributes the finality of eternal stasis - not mere cold, but the complete cessation of all possibility. Each strike doesn't freeze the victim's body, but freezes their very concept of existence, locking them in perfect, unchanging torment that becomes their new definition of reality.

The Toxic Sword injects something far worse than poison - it spreads Ven's omniversal perspective like a virus through the victim's consciousness. They begin to perceive reality as Ven does: a condemned prisoner begging for mercy. The toxin doesn't kill - it authors new definitions of pain that transcend physical limitations, rewriting the victim's very understanding of suffering.

Together, they create a weapon that embodies Ven's nature as the Omniversal Overseer - a blade that doesn't simply execute, but rewrites the fundamental laws of existence to ensure that death becomes an appeal that can never be heard.
