Forbidden Sword

The Twin Obscenities

+++

(hastesword, etherealsword, ghostsword, drainsword)

Forged in the moment when reality first learned to fear itself, these twin blades were born from the fusion of four cursed weapons: the Hastesword that moved faster than time, the Etherealsword that cut through dimensions, the Ghostsword that severed souls from flesh, and the Drainsword that fed on life itself. When the arcane fusion process split the combined essence, it created not one weapon, but two - a matched pair of horrors that exist in perfect, twisted harmony.

The first blade, Whisper, embodies speed and ethereal cutting - it phases between dimensions at velocities that make light seem sluggish, slicing through armor, flesh, and spirit simultaneously. Its edge exists in multiple planes at once, ensuring that no defense can truly block its passage.

The second blade, Hunger, manifests the draining and ghostly aspects - it doesn't just wound, but feeds voraciously on everything it touches: blood, soul energy, memories, even the victim's will to exist. Each cut leaves the target weaker not just physically, but spiritually diminished.

Together, they create a combat style of obscene efficiency: Whisper strikes faster than perception can follow, opening wounds across multiple dimensional layers, while Hunger immediately feeds on what spills forth. The wielder moves in a dance of impossible speed and insatiable consumption, leaving behind only desiccated husks drained of everything that once made them alive.

They are called Obscenities because they violate the fundamental rules of both warfare and death - turning combat into a feeding frenzy that offends existence itself.
