Background

In the forgotten-ages, where the veils between gods and mortals were thinner.

Than the blood in one's veins, there arose a warrior who defied the very essence of mortality.

Not a champion of any-cause, nor a savior of any realms.

When civilizations were as nothing more than whispers in the dust.

Realms both in the living and the dead.

Slipping between-worlds as one might walk.

A being who transcends the boundaries of both life and death.

Not crafted by mortal hands, nor brought into existence through-divine means.
